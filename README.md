## ⚡ From Raw Dictation to Professional Document in 1 Second

Tired of paying hefty monthly subscription fees for voice tools ($360/year) or dealing with cloud privacy risks? 

**InstantFlow** is a 100% Local & Private AI voice dictation and text formatting desktop application designed for power users, developers, and creators. It doesn't just transcribe your voice—it eliminates the tedious post-editing phase entirely.

### 🎯 The Core Innovation: "Format Mode"
While traditional tools provide accurate but chaotic raw transcripts, InstantFlow introduces **Format Mode**. With just one click, it intelligently restructures, formats, and polishes your spoken words into a clean, professional document outline ready for deployment.

* **🔒 100% Local & Private:** Your voice, your data. No cloud processing, zero privacy leaks.
